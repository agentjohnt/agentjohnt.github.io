Mission Statement

We are of one mind, passion and integrity. Devoted and dedicated to sensitising, enlightening and encouraging Nigerians on a future hope for the nation. We believe we have a goal, our responsibility to rise again and together hold back that which had torn apart, build back desolate dreams with one voice and hope in our hearts; the center which holds. And with our hands we work diligently and our feet shod with good tidinngs and inspirations of wisdom. We are DE Young Movement Nigeria; A rising sun from the midst!

Vision Statement

To foster Patriotism among the people. Enhance a Spirit of Unism and love among the people. Remind the people of the Nation; how we got here. Enlightening the people to see solutions to emerged problems and resuscitate them. Make leaders and the led awake tohuge responsibility. To foster transparent leadership and uncorrupt intentional motives. To make the people of the nation understand it takes selfless living to make beter. To champion fair process and equality. To enhance opportunities given to the average man. To eradicate the didease of disparity, insecurity, lack of trust, hatred and every gullible act that could in turn relinquish future hope. To project honest, true and transparent leadership and followership. Raise an alarm for battling handing to the new generation. Through strategic information publicise to the masses the emergence of a new Home - Nigeria.
