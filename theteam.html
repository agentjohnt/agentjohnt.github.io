Meet our Team

Abah Christian OCHE

Founder

He is a vibrant youth who loves God and has passion for kingdom business. He's an entrepreneur and adminstrator.

He had his first diploma in Mass Communication at Moshood Abiola Polytechnic (2014-2016). Then proceeded to acquire his degree in Industrial Relations and Personnel Management from Olabisi Onabanjo University (2018-2021).

While on campus, he served as the President of an interdenominational body, Worshippers Fellowship (MAPoly Chapter)

He served as a youth leader for 6 years in the Foursquare Gospel Church Sagamu District Headquarters where he served also served as the District Youth Secretary. He served as a student pastor and an evangelist on campus and moreso the administrative head of the fellowship. He also seerved under the umbrella of the National Fellowship of Foursquare Students in Nigeria aas the Ogun State Project Coordinator, to strengthen and restore, and General Secretary (2019-2021)

He has beeen a mentor and a life coach to many young fellow across the globe. He's the founder of Grace Transcent Music and Visioneer of the Trumpeter of Revival. He's also the visioneer and founder of DE-YOUNG MOVEMENT in Nigeria (A Movement Devoted and dedicated to sensitisingand encouraging Nigerianson hope for the nation and also believes is a part of the move and an agent of Transformation to bring that Hope). He's affiliated with MayConcept, MayowaowolabiConsult, lifepathcoach, Pacesetter Teens and Youth Camp.
